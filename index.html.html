Who We Are

About Perstrix

Perstrix is a student-led STEM Racing team competing in the F1 in Schools Development Class in the UAE. We unite six passionate students across engineering, design, and business disciplines to design, build, and race high-performance miniature F1 cars.

Our name originates from a fusion of the Peregrine Falcon — nature's fastest bird, symbolizing aerodynamic excellence and extreme performance — and Stryx (Latin for owl), representing intelligence and strategic thinking. Together, they embody the perfect balance between raw speed and calculated precision.

We are structured across two departments: our Engineering Dept. handles aerodynamic research, CAD modelling, CFD testing, and manufacturing. Our Enterprise Dept. manages branding, sponsorship, and marketing strategy.

Engineering Excellence

Our Car

Our car was designed using the principle of Form Follows Function — every cut, curve, and component has a calculated purpose. We designed the vehicle aerodynamics holistically, considering the entire system rather than individual parts.

Through extensive CFD analysis using ANSYS Discovery and CAD modelling in Autodesk Fusion 360, we refined our design through multiple iterations before manufacturing via CNC Router and 3D printing.

Cone Nose & Ramp Front Wings
Redirects airflow smoothly past the wheels, reducing turbulence that caused drag in our earlier teardrop wing design.
02
Rectangular Side Pods
Prevents unwanted air direction toward the wheels while balancing drag increase against improved airflow control.
03
Tapered Rear Pod & Teardrop Wing
Eliminates wake formation behind the car, ensuring stability and uniform airflow across all operating conditions.
